PVAс dispersions

PVA glue is a viscous white homogeneous liquid, which is the product of polymerization of vinyl acetate in an aquatic environment in the presence of an emulsifier and a polymerization initiator, mixed with it or without plasticizer. Our company produce PVA glue acoording to modern leading technology, which guarantees stable and high quality product. PVA glue is moslty used for woodworking, cardboard and paper, construction and other industries. According to client's request glue can be produced with various parameters.
