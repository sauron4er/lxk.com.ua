Once again, our company has confirmed compliance with the requirements of ISO 9001:2015 during the successful completion of the 2nd surveillance audit. This proves that the quality of production and products at the company comes first.

Perechin Timber-Chemical Plant also passed a social audit for compliance with the BSCI (Business Social Compliance Initiative) Code of Conduct with the highest grade of "A". Compliance with the provisions of the BSCI Code of Conduct enables all our customers to be convinced once again of the good working conditions at our company, the social interests of our employees, our ethical behaviour and our respect for the environment. We implement and uphold these values by making a commitment to the team and community in the region.

Another achievement of our company was obtaining the Nordic Swan Ecolabel licence, one of the most prestigious licences in Scandinavian and Nordic countries. The Nordic Swan Ecolabel sets high standards in environmental protection, health protection and maintaining product quality and functionality. The Nordic Swan Ecolabel licence confirms that our products are of high quality and are environmentally friendly.

In addition, the high quality of our products is confirmed by international DIN+ certificates from DIN Certco as an independent accredited certification body.

All of our achievements are the stable support of top management and the hard, professional and teamwork of the entire company staff! Constant control at all stages of production ensures the high quality and competitiveness of our products in Europe and the world, which in turn strengthens and enhances the confidence of the international consumer in our products and their quality.
