Industry

Woodworking industry:
Uf resins and PVA dispersions are widely used in woodworking industry. These products are mainly used for producing wooden boards with various waterproof properties, plywood and others wooden products. Due to modern technologies at our plant, our glues allow to produce final products with high mechanical properties and low formaldehyde emission, which meets European standards.
Cardboard and paper industry:
UF resins, PVA dispersions, and polyvinyl based glues are widely used in this industry. This products mainly used for paper impregnation, also for packaging materials production, cardboard, corrugated cardboard and wood, tubes, edge protectors, and also for stick labels production.
Light industry:
In light industry are widely used PVA dispersions for production of starching agents for household chemicals and also as additives in production of various types of cloth and leather.
Construction industry:
In costruction industry are widely used PVA dispersions as a binder in production of polymercement, polymerconcrete and other construction mixtures.
Paint and varnish industry:
In paint and varnish industry are widely used PVA dispersion mostly as a binder in production of matt and gloss water-based paints, putty, plasters and other materials.
